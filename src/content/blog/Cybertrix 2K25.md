Leading CYBERTRIX 2K25 as the Overall Event Coordinator (Event Director) was one of the most defining leadership experiences of my academic journey. Organized by the Department of Computer Science Engineering at St. Joseph’s Institute of Technology, the national-level technical symposium brought together students, innovators, and tech enthusiasts from various institutions for a day filled with competition, collaboration, and innovation.

Alongside an incredible organizing team, I was responsible for overseeing end-to-end event execution, participant coordination, operational management, and the successful handling of five major technical and non-technical events. Managing a crowd of over 1000+ participants required continuous coordination, quick decision-making, and efficient teamwork throughout the symposium.

CYBERTRIX 2K25 marked a historic achievement for our institution, becoming the first department symposium to cross the 1000+ participant milestone. The experience strengthened my abilities in leadership, large-scale event operations, strategic planning, and team management while reinforcing my passion for building impactful student communities and technical ecosystems.
