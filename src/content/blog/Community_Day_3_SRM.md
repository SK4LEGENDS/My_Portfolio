Attending “Beyond RPA: The Rise of Data-Driven Agentic Automation” at SRM IST Chennai Ramapuram was an inspiring and knowledge-packed experience focused on the future of intelligent automation. The event featured engaging technical sessions, interactive discussions, and hands-on learning opportunities centered around AI-powered automation ecosystems.

The sessions provided deep insights into Agentic Automation, Conversational AI in UiPath, and the growing role of intelligent agents in enterprise workflows. A standout experience was the practical session exploring Claude Code and AI-assisted terminal workflows, which offered valuable exposure to emerging developer tools and automation practices.

Beyond the technical learning, the event created an excellent environment for networking, collaboration, and connecting with professionals and students passionate about AI, automation, and innovation.
