Serving as the Chair of IEEE CS SBC for IEEE CS Day 2024 was a rewarding experience centered around innovation, collaboration, and community-driven learning. Hosted at St. Joseph’s Institute of Technology, the event focused on the rapidly evolving field of UI/UX Design and brought together students, faculty members, and industry professionals for an engaging in-person technical experience.

The sessions explored topics including the history of personal computing, the emergence of UI/UX design, AI-driven interfaces, ethical design practices, real-world applications, and future opportunities in front-end technologies. Interactive activities such as the Mini ICT Quiz added excitement and encouraged active participation throughout the event.

Working closely with speakers, faculty mentors, volunteers, and the IEEE CS team strengthened my abilities in leadership, event coordination, team management, and professional communication. The positive feedback from participants and the enthusiastic response toward future editions made the event a memorable milestone in my journey with IEEE and the student developer community.
