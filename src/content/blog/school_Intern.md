Conducting a technical seminar for 11th and 12th grade students was a meaningful opportunity to inspire future engineers and introduce them to emerging technologies shaping the modern digital landscape. Alongside mentors and peers, the seminar focused on creating awareness about automation, professional networking, and digital design tools that can empower students in their academic and career journeys.

As part of the session, we explored UiPath and its industrial applications, demonstrating how Robotic Process Automation is transforming workflows across industries. We also introduced students to LinkedIn, highlighting the importance of professional networking, personal branding, and building meaningful industry connections early in their careers.

The seminar further included interactive sessions on Figma and Canva, encouraging students to explore creativity, user interface design, and digital content creation. The enthusiasm and curiosity shown by the participants made the experience highly rewarding and reinforced the importance of knowledge sharing, mentorship, and technology-driven learning initiatives.
