The inauguration of PATTAYAM – My Idea, My Property marked an important milestone in promoting innovation, creativity, and intellectual property awareness within St. Joseph’s Institute of Technology. Organized by the Department of Computer Science Engineering, the initiative aimed to encourage students and innovators to understand the importance of protecting original ideas, patents, and research contributions.

The event featured insightful sessions from distinguished experts in the field of Intellectual Property Rights, providing valuable guidance on patents, innovation protection, and the growing relevance of IPR in technology and research ecosystems. A major highlight was the release of a powerful short film illustrating the importance of patents and the role of PATTAYAM in supporting innovators and creators.

Being part of this initiative provided valuable experience in interdisciplinary collaboration, event coordination, and innovation-driven community engagement while reinforcing the significance of protecting and nurturing creative ideas for future technological advancement.
