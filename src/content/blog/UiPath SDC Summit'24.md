Coordinating the UiPath SDC Summit’24 at St. Joseph’s Group of Institutions was an enriching experience in leadership, teamwork, and professional event management. Organized by the RPA Society St. Joseph’s, OMR, Chennai, the summit brought together automation professionals, mentors, and aspiring students to explore the growing impact of UiPath and Robotic Process Automation.

My responsibilities included managing event operations, coordinating speaker sessions, supporting attendee engagement, and ensuring seamless collaboration between organizers and technical teams. The opportunity to interact with experienced professionals and UiPath experts provided valuable exposure to enterprise automation practices, industry collaboration, and community-driven learning.
