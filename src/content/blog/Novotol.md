Serving as a Student Organizer for the UK-India Collaborative Workshop on Planetary Resilience was a deeply enriching experience that expanded my perspective on sustainability, interdisciplinary research, and global collaboration. Supported by the British Council and the Indian Council of Social Science Research (ICSSR), the initiative was organized by the University of Greenwich in collaboration with St. Joseph’s Institute of Technology.

The workshop focused on critical global challenges including climate change adaptation, sustainable urban development, energy transition, and community resilience. Through expert-led discussions and collaborative sessions, I gained valuable exposure to how technology, environmental awareness, and social sciences can work together to create innovative and sustainable solutions for the future.

As the Student Organizer (Secretary), I worked closely with the IEEE Student Chapter team to coordinate event activities, support delegates, and ensure smooth execution of the workshop. The experience strengthened my passion for research, sustainability, and responsible innovation while inspiring me to continue exploring impactful interdisciplinary solutions for planetary resilience.
