Hack-A-Bot 2024 was a national-level hackathon focused on intelligent automation solutions. Our team developed an AI-powered multilingual document translation service using UiPath AI Center that translated documents between multiple languages seamlessly. The solution allowed users to upload content, select a target language, and instantly receive translated documents, helping us secure 2nd place in the competition.
