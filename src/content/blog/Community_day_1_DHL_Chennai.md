Attending UiPath Community Day was an insightful experience that introduced me to the rapidly evolving landscape of Artificial Intelligence and Robotic Process Automation. The event brought together UiPath experts, MVPs, developers, and industry professionals who shared their expertise through technical sessions, live demos, and interactive discussions.

A major highlight of the event was the focus on “AI at Work,” which explored how Artificial Intelligence is transforming modern RPA workflows and enterprise automation. I also gained valuable exposure to real-world automation applications, including Ocean Freight ETA Prediction, which expanded my understanding of AI-driven business solutions.

Beyond the technical learning, the event provided an excellent platform to connect with automation professionals, exchange ideas, and gain insights into future opportunities in the RPA and AI ecosystem.
