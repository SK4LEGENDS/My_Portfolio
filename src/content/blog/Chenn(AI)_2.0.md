Being part of Chenn(AI) Version 2.0: Engineering Next-Gen Agents with UiPath was an exciting opportunity to contribute to a fast-growing community focused on Artificial Intelligence, automation, and intelligent agents. Organized in collaboration with qBotica and the UiPath Community, the event brought together students, professionals, and industry leaders to explore the future of AI-driven enterprise automation.

The sessions delivered valuable insights into AI agents, intelligent workflows, and modern automation ecosystems through engaging technical discussions and expert presentations. The event also created an inspiring environment for students to connect with industry professionals, exchange ideas, and understand emerging trends shaping the future of automation technologies.

As part of the RPA Society, contributing to this initiative strengthened my experience in community building, event coordination, and professional collaboration while reinforcing my passion for AI, automation, and technology-driven innovation.
