Attending the two-day workshop on “Futuristic Challenges & Technologies in UAVs for Sustainable Environment” at PSG College of Technology was an insightful and technically enriching experience. Conducted by SKYX Aerospace Private Limited, the workshop provided an in-depth exploration into the evolving world of UAVs, drone engineering, and sustainable technological solutions.

The sessions covered a wide range of topics including drone models, UAV construction methodologies, flight modes, payload configurations, and the critical engineering calculations involved in selecting motors, batteries, and system components for optimal drone performance. Through practical demonstrations and expert-led discussions, I gained valuable exposure to how UAV technologies can contribute to environmental monitoring, industrial operations, and sustainable innovation.

This experience significantly expanded my understanding of aerospace technologies and reinforced my interest in emerging engineering solutions focused on sustainability, automation, and real-world technological impact.
