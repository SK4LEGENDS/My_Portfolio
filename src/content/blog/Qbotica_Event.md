Attending “Unleashing UI Agents: The Future of Autonomous UI Automation with UiPath” was an inspiring experience filled with technical learning, innovation, and meaningful community interactions. The event highlighted how Agentic AI and UiPath technologies are transforming automation into intelligent systems capable of adapting, decision-making, and creating real business impact.

The sessions covered topics such as the Agentic Journey, UiPath Screenplay, Healing Agents, and intelligent document generation workflows, providing valuable insights into the future of enterprise automation. Each presentation demonstrated how autonomous UI agents are reshaping the next generation of intelligent automation solutions.

Beyond the technical sessions, the event provided an incredible opportunity to reconnect with mentors, professionals, and fellow automation enthusiasts within the UiPath and RPA ecosystem. The memorable celebration of Superstar Rajinikanth’s 75th birthday added a unique and joyful conclusion to an already impactful experience.
