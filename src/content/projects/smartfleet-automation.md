SmartFleet_Automation is an AI-powered logistics orchestration platform designed to optimize transportation dispatch operations through intelligent automation and real-time decision support systems. Built using UiPath Integration Service and enterprise-grade automation workflows, the platform combines RPA precision with AI-assisted route intelligence to streamline vehicle and driver allocation processes.

The system analyzes critical operational factors including vehicle availability, driver scheduling, traffic conditions, weather risks, and transportation constraints to generate optimized dispatch recommendations. By leveraging AI-driven evaluation pipelines, SmartFleet_Automation provides intelligent routing strategies, operational precautions, and recommendation scoring mechanisms that support efficient logistics planning and safer transportation execution.

Designed with modular workflow architecture, the platform enables scalability, maintainability, and enterprise adaptability across diverse transportation and logistics environments. By integrating automation workflows with intelligent analytics and decision-making capabilities, SmartFleet_Automation demonstrates how AI-enhanced RPA systems can transform modern fleet and logistics operations into proactive, data-driven ecosystems.
