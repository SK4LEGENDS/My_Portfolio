T.I.T.A.N. (Timetable Intelligent Technology & Agentic Negotiator) is an advanced AI-driven academic scheduling platform designed to eliminate the complexity of manual timetable generation through intelligent multi-agent negotiation systems. Built using React, FastAPI, and PydanticAI powered by Groq and Llama 3.3 70B, the platform autonomously analyzes faculty availability, subject constraints, timeslot distributions, and multi-section scheduling dependencies to generate fully balanced and conflict-free academic timetables.

The system utilizes agentic AI workflows capable of negotiating scheduling conflicts in real time while enforcing strict validation rules to prevent faculty overlaps, workload imbalance, and section-level conflicts. T.I.T.A.N. also features a premium enterprise-style interface with animated simulations, responsive dashboard experiences, real-time timetable analytics, CSV-based academic data ingestion, and centralized database management for scalable institutional deployment.

By combining autonomous reasoning systems with modern frontend engineering and scalable backend orchestration, T.I.T.A.N. transforms traditional timetable generation into an intelligent, adaptive, and enterprise-ready scheduling ecosystem.
