The AI-driven HR Analytics Agent is an enterprise-focused intelligent automation system designed to process, analyze, and orchestrate employee telemetry data through the combination of robotic process automation and Generative AI workflows. The platform analyzes workforce-related datasets including login activity, leave records, work modes, attendance patterns, and performance metrics to generate structured and actionable organizational insights.

Powered by UiPath automation and enhanced through the integration of UiPath File Analyzer (Preview), the system moves beyond traditional row-based parsing by contextually understanding HR datasets and extracting meaningful entities from Excel documents provided in PDF format. The processed information is automatically transformed into clean, structured JSON outputs optimized for downstream enterprise applications including Power BI dashboards, analytics systems, and additional RPA pipelines.

By combining intelligent document understanding, automation orchestration, and AI-assisted analytics, the platform demonstrates how enterprise HR operations can evolve into adaptive, insight-driven systems capable of supporting modern workforce intelligence and decision-making workflows.
